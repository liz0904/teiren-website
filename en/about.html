About Teiren

Teiren is a combination of Tera Byte+Siren, a security software solution that analyzes data in terabytes (TB) to become the best Siren for customers. All of the members are graduates of Korea's best next-generation security talent training program Best Of the Best (BoB), which was established to improve corporate security, solve customers' cybersecurity challenges, and solve the burden of security personnel.
Since its inception, Teiren has been listening to the challenges of various security personnel, and in practice, Teiren is now providing a security solution called SIEM by applying an advanced technology called Graph DB to address these challenges. Meet the Safe World with Teiren.
